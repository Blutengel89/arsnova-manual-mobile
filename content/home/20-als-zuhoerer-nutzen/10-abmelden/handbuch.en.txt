Title: Logout
----
Subtitle: Log out from a session
----
Steps:


1:
	Action: Tap **Sessions**
	Details:	&rarr; You are taken to the *Sessions* view.
2:
	Action: Tap **Logout**
	Details: &rarr; You are taken to the *Audience Response System* view.
3:
	Action: You are on the start page.
	Details:	You can now log in again (e.g. as teacher).
