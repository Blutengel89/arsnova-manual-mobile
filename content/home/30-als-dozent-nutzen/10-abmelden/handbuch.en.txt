Title: Logout
----
Subtitle: Log out from session
----
Steps:


1:
	Action: Tap **Sessions**
	Details:	&rarr; You are taken to the *Sessions* view.
2:
	Action: Tap **Logout**
	Details: &rarr;You are taken to the *Audience Response System* view.
3:
	Action: You are back on the main page.
	Details:	You can now log in again (e.g. as student).
