Title: Students' Questions
----
Subtitle: Answer Students' questions
----
Steps:

1:
	Action: Tap on a Session short name
	Details: &rarr; You are taken to the *Start* view. 
		
2:
	Action: Tap **Students' questions**
	Details:>
		A blue badge next to *Students' questions* shows the number of students' questions.
	
		
		&rarr; You are taken to the *Auditorium* view.
		
3:
	Action: Tap on a question
	Details:>
		**Note**&#58; In this view, you can delete all questions by tapping **Delete questions**. 

	    
	    &rarr; You are taken to the  *Details* view.
		
4:
	Action: Read and answer question
	Details: If you want to delete the questions after you have answered it, tap **Delete**.

5:
	Action:  Tap **Questions**
	Details:>
		&rarr; You are taken to the *Auditorium* view.


6:
	Action:  Tap **Back**
	Details:>
			Here, you may now select another question and answer it. 


			To delete all questions, tap **Delete questions**.
	 
	  		 &rarr; You are taken to the *Start* view.

7:
	Action: You are back in the *Start* view
	Details:
